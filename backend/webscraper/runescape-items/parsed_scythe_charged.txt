style=\"clear:left\"></div>\n<h2><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Passive_effect\">Passive effect</span><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a href=\"/w/Scythe_of_vitur?section=2&amp;veaction=edit\" class=\"mw-editsection-visualeditor\" title=\"Edit section: Passive effect\">edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-divider\"> | </span><a href=\"/w/Scythe_of_vitur?action=edit&amp;section=2\" title=\"Edit section&#039;s source code: Passive effect\">edit source</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span></h2>\n<figure typeof=\"mw:File/Thumb\"><a href=\"/w/File:Scythe_of_vitur_attack.gif\" class=\"mw-file-description\"><img src=\"/images/thumb/Scythe_of_vitur_attack.gif/300px-Scythe_of_vitur_attack.gif?c202d\" decoding=\"async\" loading=\"lazy\" width=\"300\" height=\"238\" class=\"mw-file-element\" srcset=\"/images/Scythe_of_vitur_attack.gif?c202d 2x\" data-file-width=\"312\" data-file-height=\"248\" /></a><figcaption>A player attacks three opponents simultaneously with the Scythe of Vitur.</figcaption></figure>\n<p>When the player attacks with a scythe, their attack range is increased to a 1x3 arc radius in front of the player, which can allow them to hit three 1x1 targets in front of them. When attacking a large creature (a monster that occupies more than two <a href=\"/w/Game_square\" title=\"Game square\">game squares</a>), the scythe will instead apply as many hits as possible to them. A 2x2 monster (such as <a href=\"/w/Vardorvis\" title=\"Vardorvis\">Vardorvis</a>) will be hit twice per attack, while a 3x3 or larger monster (such as <a href=\"/w/Dragon\" title=\"Dragon\">dragons</a> and <a href=\"/w/Duke_Sucellus\" title=\"Duke Sucellus\">Duke Sucellus</a>) will be hit three times per attack.\n</p><p>These attacks' accuracy and strength rolls are rolled independently on each target, though each hit will deal 50% less damage, (rounded down), than the preceding hit. Even numbered base max hits gain an additional max hit on the 2nd hit, while every base max hit evenly divisible by 4, (e.g. 44, 48, 52), gain an additional max hit on the 3rd hit.\n</p><p>For example on a 3x3 monster, if a player has a base max hit of 47 with the scythe, their true maximum hit is 81 (47-23-11). If the player is able to increase the base max hit by 1 to 48, the player would get a true maximum hit of 84 (48-24-12). \n</p><p>The scythe is essentially at its most optimal performance when fighting 3x3 or larger monsters. While it can deal more damage than a <a href=\"/w/Noxious_halberd\" title=\"Noxious halberd\">noxious halberd</a> against 2x2 or smaller enemies, missing out on a third hit significantly mitigates its capabilities, and the scythe will lose out to a <a href=\"/w/Soulreaper_axe\" title=\"Soulreaper axe\">soulreaper axe</a> with five soul stacks.\n</p><p>Despite the reduced damage from the second and third hits, the scythe is still immensely powerful against large creatures, especially if they are weak to slash or have low Defence levels.\n</p>\n<h2><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Charging_and_degradation\">Charging and degradation</span><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a href=\"/w/Scythe_of_vitur?section=3&amp;veaction=edit\" class=\"mw-editsection-visualeditor\" title=\"Edit section: Charging and degradation\">edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-divider\"> | </span><a href=\"/w/Scythe_of_vitur?action=edit&amp;section=3\" title=\"Edit section&#039;s source code: Charging and degradation\">edit source</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The scythe may be charged to drastically increase its stats. In order to charge it, players must add at least one <a href=\"/w/Vial_of_blood\" title=\"Vial of blood\">vial of blood</a> and a set of 200 <a href=\"/w/Blood_rune\" title=\"Blood rune\">blood runes</a> to the <a href=\"/w/Vyre_well\" title=\"Vyre well\">vyre well</a> next to the bank in <a href=\"/w/Ver_Sinhaza\" title=\"Ver Sinhaza\">Ver Sinhaza</a>. This can be done without completing any quests in the Darkmeyer series. Additionally, once <a href=\"/w/Sins_of_the_Father\" title=\"Sins of the Father\">Sins of the Father</a> quest is completed, there is a <a href=\"/w/Vyre_well\" title=\"Vyre well\">vyre well</a> outside the bank in <a href=\"/w/Darkmeyer\" title=\"Darkmeyer\">Darkmeyer</a>. \n</p><p>Each vial and 200 blood runes adds 100 charges to the scythe, with it being able to hold up to 20,000 charges (costing <span class=\"coins coins-pos\">592.84</span> coins per attack, and a total of <span class=\"coins coins-pos\">11,856,800</span> to fully charge it with 40,000 <a href=\"/w/Blood_rune\" title=\"Blood rune\">blood runes</a> and 200 <a href=\"/w/Vial_of_blood\" title=\"Vial of blood\">vials of blood</a>). One charge is deducted per attack, as long as one of the hits deals damage. The hourly rate of using the scythe is <span class=\"coins coins-pos\">711,408</span> coins.  The fully charged scythe will last for 16.66 hours of continuous combat before reverting to its uncharged form.\n</p><p>Once the charges are added to the scythe, they can be returned by using the scythe on the vyre wells in Ver Sinhaza should players decide to uncharge the scythe. Charges left will be rounded down at 100 hits per charge; for example, with 1,275 hits remaining, 1,200 charges will be deposited. These charges may then be withdrawn from the well to receive the blood runes and vials of blood back. Uncharging outside the well, however, will not return blood runes or vials of blood.\n</p><p>In the event of a PvP death, if the scythe is not protected, all charges will be lost and the person who gets the scythe will not receive any blood runes or vials of blood.\n</p>\n<h2><span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Ornament_kit\">Ornament kit</span><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[</span><a href=\"/w/Scythe_of_vitur?section=4&amp;veaction=edit\" class=\"mw-editsection-visualeditor\" title=\"Edit section: Ornament kit\">edit</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-divider\"> | </span><a href=\"/w/Scythe_of_vitur?action=edit&amp;section=4\" title=\"Edit section&#039;s source code: Ornament kit\">edit source</a><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The Scythe of Vitur can be combined with a <a href=\"/w/Holy_ornament_kit\" title=\"Holy ornament kit\">holy ornament kit</a> to make a <a href=\"/w/Holy_Scythe_of_Vitur\" class=\"mw-redirect\" title=\"Holy Scythe of Vitur\">holy Scythe of Vitur</a>. Alternatively, the Scythe of Vitur can be combined with a <a href=\"/w/Sanguine_ornament_kit\" title=\"Sanguine ornament kit\">Sanguine ornament kit</a> to make a <a href=\"/w/Sanguine_Scythe_of_Vitur\" class=\"mw-redirect\" title=\"Sanguine Scythe of Vitur\">Sanguine Scythe of Vitur</a>. These cosmetic enhancements do not change the stats in any way, but the <a href=\"/w/Holy_Scythe_of_Vitur\" class=\"mw-redirect\" title=\"Holy Scythe of Vitur\">holy Scythe of Vitur</a> does count as a <a href=\"/w/Saradomin\" title=\"Saradomin\">Saradomin</a> item in the <a href=\"/w/God_Wars_Dungeon\" title=\"God Wars Dungeon\">God Wars Dungeon</a>.\n</p>\n<h2><span class=\"mw-headline\"
